Chocolate Cake

A rich and indulgent chocolate cake layered with silky chocolate ganache and topped with a glossy chocolate drip. Perfect for birthdays, celebrations, or whenever you crave chocolate.

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 1 cup fresh cream

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and baking soda.
  3. In another bowl, whisk eggs and sugar until smooth.
  4. Add oil, milk, and vanilla essence.
  5. Combine wet and dry ingredients to form a batter.
  6. Pour into a greased cake tin and bake for 30–35 minutes.
  7. Allow the cake to cool completely.
  8. Heat cream and pour over chopped dark chocolate.
  9. Mix until smooth to prepare the ganache.
  10. Spread ganache over the cake and create a chocolate drip effect.
  11. Refrigerate for 20 minutes before serving.

Cooking Time

Preparation: 20 minutes
Baking: 35 minutes
Chilling: 20 minutes
Total Time: 75 minutes

Serving

Serves 8–10 people. Best enjoyed with coffee, tea, or a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
