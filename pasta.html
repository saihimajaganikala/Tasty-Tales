Classic Italian Pasta

A delicious Italian-style penne pasta tossed in a rich tomato sauce, topped with grated cheese and fresh herbs. Perfect for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 250g penne pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cups tomato puree
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • 1 tsp mixed Italian herbs
  • Salt to taste
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a large pot and add salt.
  2. Cook the penne pasta until al dente and drain.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté onions until translucent.
  4. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  5. Add tomato puree, chili flakes, Italian herbs, salt, and pepper.
  6. Simmer the sauce for 8–10 minutes.
  7. Add the cooked pasta and mix well until coated.
  8. Cook for another 2 minutes.
  9. Serve hot topped with Parmesan cheese and parsley.

Cooking Time

Preparation: 10 minutes
Cooking: 20 minutes
Total Time: 30 minutes
