Chicken Curry

A flavorful Indian chicken curry cooked with aromatic spices, onions, tomatoes, and herbs. This comforting dish pairs perfectly with rice, naan, or roti.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cleaned and cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 cup water
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté chopped onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
  4. Add turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, and salt.
  5. Mix well and cook the masala for 3–4 minutes.
  6. Add chicken pieces and coat them evenly with the masala.
  7. Cook for 5 minutes on medium heat.
  8. Add water, cover, and simmer for 25–30 minutes.
  9. Sprinkle garam masala and cook for another 2 minutes.
  10. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.

Cooking Time

Preparation: 15 minutes
Cooking: 35 minutes
Total Time: 50 minutes

Serving Suggestion

Serve hot with steamed rice, jeera rice, naan, chapati, or parotta for a delicious and satisfying meal.
